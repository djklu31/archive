Sleazy Rydhaz


The last time you were out walking around the town, you may have noticed a sticker posted on a streetlight or wall that bore the words "Sleazy Rydahz." Most likely it was put there by someone who calls this nomadic band of guerrilla skaters and designers home. Note: If you want to show your support, the booty shorts make a great gender-neutral gift.
